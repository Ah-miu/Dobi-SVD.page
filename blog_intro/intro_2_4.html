Optimal Approach


At the Module Level (XW=A)

EYM-Theorem shows that directly truncating activation gives $A_k$, the optimal k-rank approximation of $A$.



At the Model Level

Performance comparison of directly truncating activations and truncating weights[1]. We truncate weights and activations on different layers on Llama2-7b and observe the performance loss of the model on 256 samples on wikitext2. In any case, truncating activations has a smaller performance loss than truncating weights.
