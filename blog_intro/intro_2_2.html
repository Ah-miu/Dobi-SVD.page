Another kind of is activation-aware based SVD: They borrow the idea from quantization and use a scaling matrix S (like the HR) to capture the varying importance of input channels and to estimate activation influence by performing SVD on WS and reconstructing W using the inverse of S matrix.

Additionally, the inverse of S matrix sometimes fails due to floating point precision issues. Different GPUs have different floating point precisions (sometimes HR may not show us the correct data).



Are they optimal? Do we have a better way?
